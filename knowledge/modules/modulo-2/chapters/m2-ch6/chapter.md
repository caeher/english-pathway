## Present Simple vs Past Simple — Comparación Integral

### Formación en comparación
| | Present Simple | Past Simple |
|--|----------------|-------------|
| **Afirmativa** | I work / She works | I worked / She went |
| **Negativa** | I don't work / She doesn't work | I didn't work / She didn't go |
| **Pregunta** | Do you work? / Does she work? | Did you work? / Did she go? |

### Marcadores temporales
| Present Simple | Past Simple |
|----------------|-------------|
| **every day/week** | yesterday, last week |
| **always, usually, often** | ago (2 years ago) |
| **in the morning** (routine) | in 2020, when I was young |
| **today** (si es habitual) | this morning (ya pasó) |

### Regla de oro
- **Present Simple** = rutinas, hechos, verdades, hábitos → *I **eat** breakfast at 7.*
- **Past Simple** = acciones completadas en el pasado → *I **ate** breakfast at 7 **yesterday**.*

### Ejemplos lado a lado
- She **works** in a bank. (trabaja — rutina) vs She **worked** in a bank. (trabajó — ya no)
- He **goes** to the gym **every day**. vs He **went** to the gym **yesterday**.
- Water **boils** at 100°C. (hecho) vs The water **boiled** yesterday. (pasó)

> 💡 Cuando hay un **marcador de pasado** (yesterday, last week, ago), usa **past simple**.
