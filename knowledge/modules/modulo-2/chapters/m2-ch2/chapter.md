## Frequency Adverbs & Daily Routines

### Escala de frecuencia
| Adverbio | Frecuencia | Ejemplo |
|----------|-----------|---------|
| **always** | 100% | *I always brush my teeth.* |
| **usually** | 90% | *I usually have breakfast.* |
| **often** | 70% | *She often reads books.* |
| **sometimes** | 50% | *We sometimes eat out.* |
| **rarely / seldom** | 10% | *He rarely watches TV.* |
| **never** | 0% | *I never smoke.* |

### Posición en la oración
- **Antes** del verbo principal: *I **always** eat breakfast.*
- **Después** del verbo "to be": *She **is** always happy.*

### Expresiones de frecuencia
- every day / every week / every month / every year
- once a week / twice a month / three times a year
- on Mondays / in the morning / at night

> 💡 **Tip:** "Never" ya es negativo: *I never smoke.* (NO ~~I don't never smoke~~)
