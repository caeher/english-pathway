## Negociación y Persuasión — El arte del acuerdo en inglés

En los negocios, no basta con tener razón. Hay que **comunicar con diplomacia** y encontrar soluciones que funcionen para todos.

### Fases de una Negociación

#### 1. 📋 Proponer
| Frase | Nivel |
|---|---|
| "I'd like to suggest..." | Formal |
| "How about if we...?" | Semi-formal |
| "What if we considered...?" | Formal/tentativo |
| "Perhaps we could explore..." | Muy formal |

#### 2. ✅ Aceptar condiciones
| Frase | Nivel |
|---|---|
| "That works for us." | Neutro |
| "We can agree to that." | Formal |
| "That sounds fair." | Neutro |
| "I think we have a deal." | Semi-formal |

#### 3. ❌ Rechazar diplomáticamente
| Frase | Nivel |
|---|---|
| "I'm afraid that won't work for us." | Diplomático |
| "That's not quite what we had in mind." | Suave |
| "We'd need to reconsider that." | Formal |
| "I appreciate the offer, but..." | Cortés |

#### 4. 🤝 Buscar compromiso
| Frase | Nivel |
|---|---|
| "Could we meet halfway?" | Directo pero diplomático |
| "What if we compromised on...?" | Tentativo |
| "Perhaps we could find a middle ground." | Formal |
| "Let's see if there's a solution that works for both of us." | Colaborativo |

#### 5. 📝 Cerrar acuerdo
| Frase | Nivel |
|---|---|
| "So we've agreed that..." | Resumen |
| "Let me summarize what we've discussed." | Formal |
| "Shall we put this in writing?" | Próximo paso |

### Técnicas de Persuasión

| Técnica | Cómo | Ejemplo |
|---|---|---|
| **Ethos** (credibilidad) | Citar experiencia o autoridad | "Based on our 10 years of experience..." |
| **Pathos** (emoción) | Apelar a sentimientos | "Imagine how this could improve your team's morale..." |
| **Logos** (lógica) | Usar datos y razón | "The data clearly shows a 30% improvement..." |

### Hedging — Suavizar afirmaciones

En negociaciones, el lenguaje directo puede parecer agresivo. Usa **hedging**:

| Directo ❌ | Suavizado ✅ |
|---|---|
| "That's wrong." | "I see it slightly differently." |
| "We can't do that." | "That might be challenging for us." |
| "You need to..." | "It might be worth considering..." |
| "I disagree." | "I appreciate your perspective, however..." |

> 💡 **Tip:** En negociaciones, la diplomacia no es debilidad — es estrategia. El objetivo es ganar el acuerdo, no la discusión.
