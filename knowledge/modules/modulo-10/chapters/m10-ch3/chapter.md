## Conectores — De la fragmentación a la fluidez

Los conectores son las "bisagras" que unen tus ideas y hacen que tu inglés suene natural y fluido.

### Compara estos textos:

**Sin conectores (fragmentado):**
> I wanted to go out. It was raining. I stayed home. I watched a movie. It was good.

**Con conectores (fluido):**
> I wanted to go out, **but** it was raining, **so** I stayed home. **Instead**, I watched a movie, **and** it was really good.

---

### Categorías de conectores

#### ➕ Adición
| Conector | Uso | Ejemplo |
|---|---|---|
| and | Unir ideas similares | I like tea **and** coffee. |
| also | Añadir información | She **also** speaks French. |
| moreover | Formal, añadir énfasis | **Moreover**, the price is very reasonable. |
| in addition | Formal | **In addition**, we offer free shipping. |

#### 🔄 Contraste
| Conector | Uso | Ejemplo |
|---|---|---|
| but | Contraste simple | I'm tired, **but** I'll keep working. |
| however | Formal, contraste fuerte | **However**, not everyone agrees. |
| although | A pesar de que | **Although** it was cold, we went outside. |
| despite | A pesar de (+ noun/-ing) | **Despite** the rain, we had fun. |

#### 🔍 Causa
| Conector | Uso | Ejemplo |
|---|---|---|
| because | Porque | I stayed home **because** I was sick. |
| since | Ya que (formal) | **Since** you're here, let's start. |
| due to | Debido a (+ noun) | **Due to** the weather, the event was canceled. |

#### ➡️ Resultado
| Conector | Uso | Ejemplo |
|---|---|---|
| so | Así que | It was raining, **so** I took an umbrella. |
| therefore | Por lo tanto (formal) | **Therefore**, we need to change our plan. |
| as a result | Como resultado | **As a result**, sales increased. |

#### 🔢 Secuencia
| Conector | Uso | Ejemplo |
|---|---|---|
| first | Primero | **First**, preheat the oven. |
| then / next | Luego | **Then**, add the ingredients. |
| after that | Después de eso | **After that**, mix everything. |
| finally | Finalmente | **Finally**, bake for 30 minutes. |

> 💡 **Tip:** Usar conectores avanzados como *however*, *moreover* y *therefore* hace que tu inglés suene más sofisticado y profesional.
