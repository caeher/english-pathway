## Numbers, Days & Months — Números, Días y Meses

### Números cardinales (1-20)
1 one, 2 two, 3 three, 4 four, 5 five,
6 six, 7 seven, 8 eight, 9 nine, 10 ten,
11 eleven, 12 twelve, 13 thirteen, 14 fourteen, 15 fifteen,
16 sixteen, 17 seventeen, 18 eighteen, 19 nineteen, 20 twenty

### Decenas (10-100)
10 ten, 20 twenty, 30 thirty, 40 forty, 50 fifty,
60 sixty, 70 seventy, 80 eighty, 90 ninety, 100 one hundred

### Números ordinales (para fechas)
1st first, 2nd second, 3rd third, 4th fourth, 5th fifth, 6th sixth, 7th seventh, 8th eighth, 9th ninth, 10th tenth…
20th twentieth, 21st twenty-first, 22nd twenty-second, 31st thirty-first

### Formato de fechas
- **March 5th, 2026** — 5 de marzo de 2026
- **The 5th of March** — El 5 de marzo
- **My birthday is on March 5th.** — Mi cumpleaños es el 5 de marzo.

### Days of the Week — Días de la semana
Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday, Friday, Saturday, Sunday

### Months of the Year — Meses del año
January, February, March, April, May, June,
July, August, September, October, November, December

### Telling the Time — Decir la hora
- **It's 3 o'clock.** — Son las 3 en punto.
- **It's 3:15.** — It's quarter past three. (y cuarto)
- **It's 3:30.** — It's half past three. (y media)
- **It's 3:45.** — It's quarter to four. (menos cuarto)
- **5:10** — ten past five / five ten
- **6:45** — quarter to seven / six forty-five
- **9:20** — twenty past nine / nine twenty
- **11:55** — five to twelve / eleven fifty-five

> 💡 **Tip:** Los días y meses siempre se escriben con **mayúscula** en inglés.
