### Formation: Subject + am/is/are + verb-ing
| Subject | Be | Verb-ing | Example |
|---------|-----|----------|---------|
| I | **am** | working | I **am working** now. |
| He / She / It | **is** | reading | She **is reading** a book. |
| You / We / They | **are** | playing | They **are playing** football. |

### Contractions
- I**'m** working / She**'s** reading / They**'re** playing

### Uses
1. **Action happening NOW:** I'm studying right now.
2. **Temporary situation:** She's living in Madrid this month.
3. **Future plans:** We're meeting tomorrow at 5.
4. **Trends/Changes:** Prices are rising.

### Negative & Questions
- **Negative:** I'm **not** working. / She **isn't** reading.
- **Question:** **Are** you working? / **Is** she reading?
- **Short:** Yes, I am. / No, she isn't.
