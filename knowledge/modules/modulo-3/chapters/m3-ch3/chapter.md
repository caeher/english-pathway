## Past Continuous — Pasado Continuo

### Formación: Subject + was/were + verb-ing
| Subject | Was/Were | Verb-ing | Example |
|---------|----------|----------|---------|
| I / He / She / It | **was** | working | I **was working** at 9 PM. |
| You / We / They | **were** | studying | They **were studying** all night. |

### Negative & Questions
- **Neg:** I **wasn't** working. / They **weren't** studying.
- **Q:** **Was** she working? / **Were** they studying?
- **Short:** Yes, I was. / No, they weren't.

### Uses
1. **Action in progress at a specific past time:**
   - At 8 PM, I **was watching** TV.
2. **Two simultaneous actions (while):**
   - **While** she was cooking, he **was reading**.
3. **Background action interrupted (when + Past Simple):**
   - I **was walking** home **when** it started to rain.
   - She **was sleeping** **when** the phone rang.

### When vs. While
| Conjunction | Use | Example |
|-------------|-----|---------|
| **when** | + Past Simple (short action) | I was eating **when** he arrived. |
| **while** | + Past Continuous (long action) | **While** I was eating, he arrived. |

> 💡 **when** → corta y puntual / **while** → larga y continua
