### Negativo e Interrogativo
- **Negativo**: Subject + **didn't** + verbo **base** 
  - *"I didn't go to work."*
  - *"She didn't like the movie."*
  
- **Question**: **Did** + subject + verb **base**?
  - *"Did you see the movie?"*
  - *"Did she finish her homework?"*
