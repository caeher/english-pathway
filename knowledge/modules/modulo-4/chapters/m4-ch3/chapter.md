## Superlatives — Superlativos

### Short adjectives: the + adj-est
| Adjective | Superlative | Example |
|-----------|-------------|---------|
| tall | the tall**est** | She is **the tallest** in class. |
| big | the bigg**est** | This is **the biggest** house. |
| happy | the happi**est** | He is **the happiest** person. |

### Long adjectives: the most + adj
| Adjective | Superlative | Example |
|-----------|-------------|---------|
| beautiful | **the most** beautiful | She is **the most beautiful**. |
| expensive | **the most** expensive | This is **the most expensive** car. |
| interesting | **the most** interesting | It's **the most interesting** book. |

### Irregular Superlatives
| Adjective | Comparative | Superlative |
|-----------|-------------|-------------|
| good | better | **the best** |
| bad | worse | **the worst** |
| far | farther | **the farthest** |
| much/many | more | **the most** |
| little | less | **the least** |

### Key: Always use THE
- Mount Everest is **the** highest mountain.
- She is **the** best student in **the** class.

### Comparatives vs. Superlatives
| Compare 2 | Compare 3+ |
|-----------|------------|
| She is tall**er** than him. | She is **the** tall**est** in class. |
| This is more expensive than that. | This is **the most** expensive. |
